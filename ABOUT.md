# iFound.today

### The indie product feed. Swipe to discover tools you'll actually use.

---

## The problem

Finding good indie products is broken.

Directories turned discovery into a **popularity contest** — ranked by upvotes, won on launch day, gamed by whoever has the biggest audience or the budget to pay for placement. Great products with no marketing muscle sink to the bottom. You scroll a wall of 50 tiny thumbnails and click the same top 3 everyone else does.

## The fix

**iFound is a full-screen, swipeable feed of indie products — like TikTok, but for tools.**

One product at a time. Full screen. Shuffled fresh on every visit. No upvotes, no rankings, no pay-to-play. Every maker gets the exact same shot, and *you* decide what's good — one swipe at a time.

## How it works

- **Swipe, don't scroll.** Each product gets the entire screen with a live preview of its site — see the real thing, not a pitch.
- **Shuffled & fair.** The order is random every time. No one buys their way to the top, because there is no top.
- **One tap to the source.** Every card links straight to the maker's site. No interstitials, no tracking, no sign-up walls.

## Built for makers, not marketers

- **Free to list. Forever.** Submit through a GitHub Issue — no account, no fee, no "premium tier."
- **Verified maker badge.** Prove you built it, get a green check on your card.
- **Direct traffic.** Every visitor goes straight to your site. Zero middleman pages.

## Thousands of products, fairly surfaced

iFound aggregates **4,000+ indie products** from **Product Hunt** and **TrustMRR** (real revenue-verified startups), plus direct maker submissions — then strips away the rankings and serves them one at a time. Many cards show the maker's handle and real MRR, so you discover not just products, but the people and businesses behind them.

## Open source, no backend

The whole thing is open source and runs with **no backend and no database** — just a static feed that auto-updates daily. Fork it, run your own directory, or contribute back.
